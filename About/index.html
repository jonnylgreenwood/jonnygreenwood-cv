Hey, I'm Jonny born n' bred in sunny Leeds. I grew up a fairly nerdy kid, and honestly, that hasn’t changed much. I studied Chemistry at Sheffield Hallam University, where I discovered that I loved working with data far more than working in the lab. I graduated with a First-Class BSc (Hons), and not long after, I joined Johnson & Johnson.

There, I cultivated my love for data and found my stride in project work, bringing new tools and ideas to turn tedious analytics into something much more dynamic (a bit like taming a wild puppy and ending up with a loyal companion). After working in the EMEA demand planning team, I moved into commercial analytics, working closely with Sales and Pricing Strategy. One of my proudest moments was simplifying a notoriously complex NHS framework and, quite by accident, creating one of the best ways to model every company’s pricing structure. (NHS England — my DMs are open.)

From there, I joined the Spine End-to-End Planning team, where I touched nearly every part of the supply chain and helped plan the launch of new products.

More recently, I took a sabbatical to travel, volunteer, and live a little differently. I love an adventure — and while most people plan a beach holiday, I tend to wonder if I can get from A to B on horseback, and more importantly, who’s in charge of the music. You can follow along on my Linktree.
